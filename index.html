Stage 2: SafeSpy Snapshot

A first insight page helps the member see what is publicly visible and what it may prompt them to review. It does not diagnose root cause.

First visible view
👀

What is visible

Sample public records show a live website, standard contact routes, several third-party services, and a few older staff profile references.

💡

What it might mean

The member may benefit from checking account ownership, supplier access, and whether public contact details are still current.

🛠️

What the member can do now

Confirm who manages the domain, review administrator access, and update profile pages that no longer reflect the team.

What this snapshot cannot conclude

It cannot confirm whether systems are secure, whether contracts are suitable, or whether internal processes are working well. It only shows a narrow external view.

Recommended next step

Use the 1-1 routing conversation to decide whether the member needs a cyber follow-up, people readiness review, or a broader resilience route.

Included partner pages

Single recommended next step

Book a 45-minute surgery session to agree account ownership, review supplier access, and decide whether a contract check is needed.

Limitations / caveats

The pack is a pilot support document. It is not a certification, audit, legal opinion, or guarantee of operational resilience.
